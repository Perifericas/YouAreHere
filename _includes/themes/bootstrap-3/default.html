SHARE YOUR STORY

To experience You Are Here, visit one of our locations and join the wifi network. Then open your browser to youarehere.com, and you'll be able to listen to listen to the stories of the parks and add your own thoughts!

TOMPKINS SQUARE PARK

Tompkins Square Park can be defined by many different communities. Visit our location to hear from some of the residents in this tour from producer Hillary Brueck.

THE HIGH LINE

The High Line is the next step in the evolution of the city's many private public spaces. Visit our location to hear from some of the residents in this tour from producer Dasha Lisitsina.
